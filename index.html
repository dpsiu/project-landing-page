Our amazing wildlife

Howler monkeys are known for their loud, distinctive calls that can be heard up to three miles away, and their impressive arboreal acrobatics.

The emerald tree boa is a non-venomous snake with striking green coloration and a unique way of hanging from tree branches.

The ibex is a wild goat species known for its impressive horns and agility in navigating rocky terrain at high altitudes.

Ribbon eels are fascinating creatures with a unique appearance and behavior, including their ability to change color and gender.
